## One-Paragraph Thesis

Accenture is the world's largest professional-services firm — a wide-moat, capital-light, net-cash franchise generating ~$11B free cash flow — whose stock has fallen ~48% over the past year and now trades at roughly 12x forward adjusted EPS and a ~10% FCF yield, a 40%+ discount to its own multi-year average. The market is pricing a structural-impairment scenario in which generative AI collapses the consulting/labor-arbitrage revenue pool. The operating data so far contradicts that: record Q2 FY26 bookings of $22.1B, ~5x peer share gains, $5.9B FY25 GenAI bookings (~3x prior year), book-to-bill ~1.2, and a raised FY26 free-cash-flow guide. The headline-growth deceleration (FY26 guide +3–5% local currency vs. mid-teens in FY22) is real; only its terminal magnitude is in genuine dispute. The result is a high-asymmetry valuation setup: ~3.3:1 reward/risk with a clearly monitored thesis-break trigger.

## Moat & BAIT

- **Moat: Wide** — switching costs (195/200 top clients 10+ yrs), scale/breadth (~786k people), ecosystem intangibles, internally-funded reinvestment flywheel. Vulnerability: AI compressing headcount-based delivery value.
- **BAIT: 3–4 lens overlap.** Behavioral STRONG (sentiment vs. operating-data divergence), Analytical / Informational / Technical MODERATE.
