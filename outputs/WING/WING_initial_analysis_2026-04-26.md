## Executive Summary

Wingstop is a best-in-class asset-light franchise royalty business (98% franchised; 6% royalty rate; $2.0M domestic AUV — best-in-class for emerging QSR) whose first-ever same-store-sales decline in 22 years (FY2025: –3.3%; Q4 2025: –5.8%) drove a –51% drawdown from $388 to $189. Three signals captured in this retrofit materially shift the thesis vs. the prior v2.5 read: (1) a fresh **$300M share-repurchase authorization** with Q4 buying executed at **$241.65 average** — well above current $189 — the highest-fidelity internal-conviction signal available; (2) **Smart Kitchen technology fully deployed across all 2,586 domestic restaurants** in 2025 (previously rolling out), with 15% delivery-time reduction achieved and a near-term frequency-lift catalyst; (3) sell-side has bridged the trough — **27 analysts cover with Strong-Buy consensus, median target $310, 24 of 27 at Buy or Strong-Buy**. Combined with **14.45% short interest** (elevated; squeeze potential), the binary Q1 2026 SSS print on April 29 sets up an asymmetric catalyst.

**PW EV: ~$230 (18-month) → +22% / ~+14% annualized. R/R: 2.8:1 (Bull / Bear).** This is a meaningfully better setup than the prior v2.5 PW EV of +5%, but the asymmetry remains contingent on the Q1 print 3 days away.
