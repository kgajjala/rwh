# Axcelis — $100M Increase to Share Repurchase Authorization — 2025-03-12

**Sources**:
- [PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/axcelis-announces-100-million-increase-to-share-repurchase-authorization-302399471.html)
- [Stocktitan recap (notes total authorization $215M)](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/ACLS/axcelis-announces-100-million-increase-to-share-repurchase-44yx0quwue96.html)

## Details
- Board approved additional **$100M** in repurchase authorization
- Brings total outstanding authorization to **$215M** (~5.2% of shares at then-prices)
- Mechanism: open market and/or privately negotiated transactions; Rule 10b-18 / 10b5-1 plans
- Company stated intent to **increase quarterly repurchase pace** while preserving balance sheet strength
- No dividend; buyback is the sole return-of-capital tool
