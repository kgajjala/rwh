## Deal Terms
- **Structure**: All-stock merger
- **Exchange ratio**: 0.3575 ACLS shares per VECO share
- **Combined EV**: ~$4.4B (using Sep 30, 2025 closing prices + outstanding debt as of Jun 30, 2025)
- **Pro-forma ownership**: ACLS holders ~58%, VECO holders ~42% (fully diluted)
- **Expected close**: 2H 2026, subject to regulatory approvals

## Pro-Forma FY2024
- Combined revenue: $1.7B
- Non-GAAP gross margin: 44%
- Adjusted EBITDA: $387M

## Strategic Rationale
- Becomes **fourth-largest US WFE supplier** (per Semiconductor Today framing)
- Complementary portfolios: ACLS implant + Veeco MOCVD/laser annealing/wet etch/ion-beam deposition
- Long-term drivers: AI, electrification, next-gen device architectures
- R&D scale; cost + revenue synergies (not yet quantified)
