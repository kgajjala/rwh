# Axcelis Q4 2025 / FY2025 Results — 2026-02-17

**Source**: [PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/axcelis-announces-financial-results-for-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-302689906.html) | [Axcelis IR](https://investor.axcelis.com/news-releases/news-release-details/axcelis-announces-financial-results-fourth-quarter-and-full-19) | [Power Semiconductors Weekly recap](https://www.powersemiconductorsweekly.com/2026/02/17/axcelis-posts-q4-2025-results-above-outlook-cites-record-csi-revenue-and-merger-progress-with-veeco/)

## Q4 2025
- Revenue: $238M (above outlook ~$215M)
- GAAP gross margin: 47.0% / non-GAAP 47.3%
- GAAP op margin: 15.2% / non-GAAP 21.1%
- GAAP diluted EPS: $1.10 / non-GAAP $1.49 (vs $1.12 cons)
- Record CS&I quarter (installed-base annuity strengthening)

## FY2025
- Revenue: $839.0M (–17.6% YoY vs FY2024 $1,017.9M)
- GAAP gross margin: 44.9% / non-GAAP 45.2% (+30bps YoY)
- Adjusted EBITDA: $177M (21.1% margin)
- GAAP EPS: $3.80 / non-GAAP $4.88

## End-Market Commentary
- **Power/SiC**: muted. Industry prioritizing 150mm→200mm wafer-size transition; SiC tool demand pushed out
- **Memory (DRAM/HBM)**: sequentially improving, AI infrastructure-driven; primary FY2026 growth driver
- **General Mature**: mixed conditions; China digesting prior mature-node investment

## Q1 2026 Guide
- Revenue ~$195M
- GAAP EPS ~$0.38
- Non-GAAP EPS ~$0.71
- *Includes ongoing Veeco transaction + integration costs*

## FY2026 Outlook
- Relatively flat vs FY2025
- Memory growth offsets power + general mature decline

## Strategic
- Veeco merger pending (announced Oct 1, 2025; shareholders approved Feb 2026; expected close 2H 2026)
