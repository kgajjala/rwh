# Amazon FY2021 Shareholder Letter (Andy Jassy — first letter as CEO)

**Source URL**: https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/company-news/2021-letter-to-shareholders
**Fiscal Year Covered**: FY2021

## Key verbatim quotes

- "this is the first time I've had the honor of writing our annual shareholder letter as CEO of Amazon."
- "Iterative innovation creates magic for customers. Constantly inventing and improving products has a compounding effect on customer experience."
- "We spent Amazon's first 25 years building a very large fulfillment network, and then had to double it in the last 24 months to meet customer demand." (COVID-era infrastructure overbuild)
