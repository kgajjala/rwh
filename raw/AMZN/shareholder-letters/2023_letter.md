# Amazon FY2023 Shareholder Letter (Andy Jassy)

**Source URL**: https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/company-news/amazon-ceo-andy-jassy-2023-letter-to-shareholders
**Fiscal Year Covered**: FY2023

## Key verbatim quotes

- "By the end of 2023, we saw cost optimization attenuating, new deals accelerating, customers renewing at larger commitments over longer time periods." (AWS reacceleration signal)
- "These AWS services, at all three layers of the stack, comprise a set of primitives that democratize this next seminal phase of AI." (3-layer GenAI strategy)
- "In 2023, for the first time since 2018, we reduced our cost to serve on a per unit basis globally." (cost-to-serve inflection)
- "We've challenged every closely held belief in our fulfillment network, and reevaluated every part of it, and found several areas where we believe we can lower costs even further while also delivering faster."
