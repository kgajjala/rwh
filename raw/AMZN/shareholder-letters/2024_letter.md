# Amazon FY2024 Shareholder Letter (Andy Jassy, published April 2025)

**Source URL**: https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/company-news/amazon-ceo-andy-jassy-2024-letter-to-shareholders
**Fiscal Year Covered**: FY2024

## Key verbatim quotes

- "Generative AI is going to reinvent virtually every customer experience we know, and enable altogether new ones about which we've only fantasized."
- "If your mission is to make customers' lives better and easier every day, and you believe every customer experience will be reinvented by AI, you're going to invest deeply and broadly in AI."
- "We only start monetizing this capital investment many months after we spend the capital, and over many years — which leads to attractive long-term FCF and ROIC."
- "AWS revenue increased 19% YoY, from $91B to $108B" and "our AI revenue is growing at triple digit YoY percentages and represents a multi-billion-dollar annual revenue run rate."
- "Speed disproportionately matters for every business, in every industry, at all times. It's a false binary to argue that you can move fast or deliver high standards."
- "Trainium2 chips offer 30-40% better price-performance than the current GPU-powered compute instances generally available today."
