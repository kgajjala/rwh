KEY VERBATIM QUOTES:

Bom Kim on monthly recovery trajectory:
"Each month since [January] has improved on a year-over-year basis and the pace of improvement strengthened through February and March."

Bom Kim on WOW membership recovery:
"Through the end of April, we've closed nearly 80% of the decline in WOW memberships that followed the incident."

Bom Kim on not dismantling capacity:
"We're not going to dismantle [capacity] for temporary disruptions; rather, the company will absorb that temporary underutilization knowing that as growth recovers demand catches up."

Bom Kim on Taiwan cohort behavior:
"Cohort retention behavior is reminiscent of what we saw in the early years of Product Commerce in Korea."

Bom Kim on Taiwan last-mile:
"Our own last-mile delivery network...covers the vast majority of our volume and that coverage continues to expand."

Gaurav Anand on WOW metrics:
"New WOW sign-ups and churn have returned to historical stable levels."

Gaurav Anand on voucher accounting:
"From an accounting perspective, the vouchers are netted against the revenue. So they did have an impact this quarter on both revenue growth and margins."

Gaurav Anand on Q2 guidance:
"For Q2, we anticipate consolidated constant currency revenue growth of 9% to 10%."
"We expect consolidated adjusted EBITDA margin year-over-year contraction of approximately 300 to 400 basis points for Q2."

Gaurav Anand on confidence in recovery:
"The levels of service and value we are able to consistently provide to customers...give us confidence that the recovery will continue to build through the year."

Bom Kim on margin recovery timeline:
"We expect annual margin expansion to resume next year [2027]."
