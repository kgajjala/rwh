- Demand cyclicality / IT spending sensitivity
- Reliance on suppliers (NVIDIA, Intel, AMD, Broadcom)
- Competitive intensity in commodified PC business
- Geopolitical / trade risk (China supply chain)
- Concentration in large customers (hyperscalers, sovereigns)
- New language FY24/FY25: AI server demand sustainability + capex digestion risk
- Cybersecurity / data breach (boilerplate)
