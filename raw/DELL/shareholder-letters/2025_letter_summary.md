# Dell FY2025 Shareholder Letter — Michael Dell (dated May 16, 2025)

Source: https://stockholderletter.com/dell/ (aggregator copy of Dell IR letter)
Original: investors.delltechnologies.com (proxy / annual report front matter)

## Key extracts

- "GenAI is driving global growth and advancing human progress, and Dell is positioned at the forefront of this technology revolution."
- "Extremely well positioned to capture growth across every segment of our business."
- AI server shipments: $9.8B in FY25, up from $1.5B in FY24
- ISG revenue $43.6B (+29% YoY)
- $10.8B returned to shareholders in FY25 ($7.5B repurchases + $3.3B dividends)
- Dividend raised 18% to $2.10 (FY25)
- Long-term commitment: return 80%+ of adjusted FCF
- Target: at least 10% annual dividend growth through FY2028
- Five new AI-optimized platforms added (Blackwell architectures); first in world to ship NVIDIA NVL-72 GB200
- "From AI server optimization to data center optimization for our largest customers"

## Pattern note

Dell follows **Pattern C** (CLAUDE.md Rule #19) — Michael Dell publishes a single
"Open Letter to Shareholders" annually as part of the proxy / annual report
front matter, typically dated mid-May for the fiscal year ended late January.
Prior years' letters available at dell.com/learn/us/en/uscorp1/corporate_secure_dellwebpage/shareholder-letter
(URL pattern, not always indexed). Aggregator: stockholderletter.com/dell/.
