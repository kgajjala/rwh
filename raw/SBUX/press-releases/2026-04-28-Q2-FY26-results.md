# Starbucks Reports Q2 Fiscal Year 2026 Results

**Reported**: April 28, 2026 (pre-market)
**Period**: Q2 FY2026 (quarter ending ~Mar 29, 2026)

## Headline Results

- **Revenue**: $9.5B (+9% Y/Y; +8% constant currency) vs. $9.12–9.16B consensus
- **GAAP EPS**: $0.45 vs. $0.42 estimate
- **Adjusted EPS**: $0.50 vs. $0.44 estimate (+22% Y/Y)
- **Operating margin**: 9.4% (+110 bps Y/Y)

## Comparable Sales

- **Global comp**: +6.2%
- **U.S. comp**: +7.1% (vs. ~2% declines in prior quarters)
- **U.S. transactions**: +4.3% (some sources +4.4%) — **second consecutive quarter of traffic growth**
- **U.S. average ticket**: ~+2.7%
- **International comp**: +2.6% — all 10 largest international markets positive comparable sales for the **first time in 9 quarters**
- Transaction-driven, not price-driven

## Niccol Commentary (Verbatim)

> "Our second quarter marked **the turn in our turnaround** as our 'Back to Starbucks' plan drove both top and bottom line growth."

Drivers cited:
- Cafe operations improvements
- New menu items
- Reintroduced seating (third-place experience restoration)
- **Reduced discounting** (i.e., traffic growth despite less promotional intensity — high-quality signal)

## FY2026 Raised Guidance

- **Global + US comp sales**: now **≥5%** (raised from ≥3% prior)
- **Adjusted EPS**: now **$2.25–$2.45** (raised from $2.15–$2.40)
- Consolidated revenues approximately flat Y/Y (reflecting Boyu deconsolidation)
- 600–650 net new stores globally

## Other

- Net new coffeehouses in quarter: only 11 — same-store improvements drove growth, not expansion
- Stock after-hours move: +5.5% to ~$102.68
