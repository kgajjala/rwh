# SharkNinja Q4 2025 Earnings Call — Key Quotes
Source: https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/11/sharkninja-sn-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/
Date: 2026-02-11
Speakers: Mark Barrocas (CEO), Adam Quigley (CFO)

## Tariffs & Supply Chain
- Barrocas: "Today, we have the ability to manufacture nearly 100% of our US volume outside of China."
- Barrocas (FY26 tariff baseline): minimum 20% China, 20% Vietnam, 19% Indonesia / Thailand / Malaysia / Cambodia.
- Quigley: "The first half, we expect a decent gross margin headwind driven by tariffs with slight offsets driven by all the cost optimization efforts."

## FY2026 Guidance
- Net sales growth: +10–11%
- Adj EPS: $5.90–$6.00 (+12–14% YoY)
- Adj EBITDA: $1.27–$1.28B (+12–13% YoY)
- Capex: $190–210M

## New categories
- "We plan to add two more categories to our portfolio in 2026"
- "At least two new product categories and 25 new products"
- Shark TrioGlow (skincare) global launch
- Ninja Fireside 360 (combo smokeless fire pit) — outdoor/Adventure

## International
- UK +7.3% YoY despite air-fryer category declines
- Mexico "triple-digit growth"
- 2H 2025 international +23.2% vs 1H +17.3%
- Direct-operator transitions: Nordics, Poland, Benelux done; Spain + Italy planned 2026

## Capital allocation
- **Inaugural $750M share repurchase program** authorized
- Quigley: "Ended the year with over $777 million of cash and cash equivalents, up more than 100% year over year."

## Strategic framing
- Barrocas: "2026 represents our first full year of optimization" (post supply-chain transformation)
- Barrocas: "Our most important objective this year and every year is building trust with consumers."
