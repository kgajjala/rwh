# UnitedHealth Group — Reports First Quarter 2026 Results (2026-04-21)

**Source**: https://www.unitedhealthgroup.com/newsroom/2026/2026-04-21-uhg-reports-first-quarter-2026-results.html

## Key headline figures

- **Revenue**: $111.72B (vs. $109.58B prior-year quarter, +2% YoY)
- **GAAP Net Income**: $6.28B | **GAAP EPS**: $6.90 (vs. $6.85 PY-Q1)
- **Adjusted EPS**: $7.23 (vs. ~$6.59 consensus → +9.7% beat)
- **Operating earnings**: $9.0B
- **Operating cash flow**: $8.9B (1.4× net income)
- **Medical Care Ratio (MCR)**: **83.9%** (vs. 84.8% Q1 2025) — meaningful improvement
- **Debt-to-capital**: 42.9%

## Guidance

- **FY2026 Adjusted EPS guide raised to >$18.25** (from prior >$17.75)
- **FY2026 GAAP EPS guide**: >$17.35
- Buybacks: $2B+ committed through end of Q2 2026

## Management commentary (Hemsley, CEO)

MCR improvement attributed to **pricing discipline, medical cost management, and
favorable reserve development**. Recovery characterized as ahead of plan.
