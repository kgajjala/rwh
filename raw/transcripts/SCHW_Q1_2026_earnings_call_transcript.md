## Analyst Q&A Highlights

### Trading Revenue Mix Pressure
**Q**: Revenue per trade declining despite record volume?  
**A** (Verdeschi): Traders "taking smaller positions, holding them for less duration" due to market uncertainty. Higher frequency, lower per-trade revenue = net growth in trading revenue.

### Competitive Positioning vs. JPMorgan
**Q**: JPMorgan reducing cash friction — threat?  
**A** (Wurster): Schwab already makes cash allocation "incredibly easy" through multiple channels. Upcoming agentic AI will further differentiate.

### Long-Short Lending Support
**Q**: Competitive risk from hedge fund financing?  
**A** (Verdeschi): Long-short lending is low capital intensity; Schwab has flexibility to support growing demand.
