# RKT — Rocket Companies

## Business Description

Rocket Companies (NYSE: RKT) is America's largest mortgage originator and, following its 2025 acquisition of Mr. Cooper, the nation's largest mortgage servicer. Through the 2025 Redfin acquisition, Rocket now controls a fully integrated homeownership platform spanning home search (Redfin, 100M+ monthly users), mortgage origination (#1 by volume, ~25% market share), and mortgage servicing (~$2.7T servicing portfolio post-Mr. Cooper).

**Core revenue streams:**
- **Mortgage origination** — Gain on sale (GOS) income from originating and selling loans; volume-driven; cyclically exposed to rate environment
- **Mortgage servicing** — Recurring fee income from $2.7T servicing portfolio; counter-cyclical (benefits when rates rise and refi volume falls)
- **MSR fair value changes** — Non-cash mark-to-market on $19.4B MSR asset; excluded from Adjusted Revenue
- **Redfin revenue** — Real estate media, brokerage commissions, mortgage attach (~40% of Redfin transactions)

**Operating brands:** Rocket Mortgage, Rocket Money, Rocket Homes, Redfin, Bay Equity, Mr. Cooper

## Moat Assessment

**Narrow-to-Emerging Wide**

Sources of competitive advantage:
- **Recapture rate 3× industry**: Rocket retains borrowers when they refinance or move at triple the industry average — a durable, data-driven structural advantage from its servicing relationship
- **AI operating leverage**: Half the headcount for same origination volume vs. 2020; 800K AI chats/month; 2.5x conversion from digital pre-approvals — building a cost structure competitors can't replicate at the same scale without years of investment
- **Integrated platform flywheel**: Redfin (search) → Rocket Mortgage (originate) → Mr. Cooper servicing (retain) is the first true end-to-end homeownership stack; attach rate went from 27% to 40% in 4 months — faster than anyone anticipated
- **Scale in servicing**: $2.7T portfolio generates recurring, rate-hedged income regardless of origination cycle

**Moat limitations**: Mortgage origination is a commodity at the transaction level; gain-on-sale margins are subject to intense competition; Rocket's competitive advantage is in efficiency (AI) and recapture, not pricing power.

## Pivotal Investment Question

**Is the integrated homeownership platform transformational, or is RKT still fundamentally a cyclical mortgage company in disguise?**

The bear answer: $10.6B of goodwill from acquisitions ($1.75B Redfin + $14.2B Mr. Cooper), $10.4B in senior notes, and a business model that collapses in a sustained high-rate environment.

The bull answer: The recapture flywheel, AI cost structure, and platform attach rate are compounding advantages that will be revealed as the housing market gradually unlocks — and the market is pricing this as a cyclical rather than a platform.

## Corporate Structure Note

Pre-June 2025: Class A shares (~7% economic interest); Dan Gilbert's Class D shares controlled the economics. The Up-C structure collapsed June 30, 2025 into a standard C-corp. Public float grew from ~7% to ~35%. YoY EPS comparisons require pro-forma adjustment — use Adjusted Revenue and Adjusted EBITDA for all period-to-period analysis.
