## Balance Sheet — Key Items

| Metric | FY2024 | FY2025 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| High-Cost Debt (FHLB + CDs) | ~$36B | ~$5.1B |
| High-Cost Debt Peak (2023) | ~$97B | — |
| Cash Sorting Impact | Peaked 2022–2024 | Reversed/resolved 2H2025 |

*The most important balance sheet story: FHLB and CD debt peaked at ~$97B in 2023 when
clients fled to money market funds. Schwab was forced to borrow expensively to fund
balance sheet. By Q4 2025, this high-cost debt was reduced to $5.1B — a massive
deleveraging that directly flows through to net interest margin improvement.*
