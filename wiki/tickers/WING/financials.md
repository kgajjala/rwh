## Balance Sheet Notes

- WING has negative book equity — a result of leveraged recapitalizations and franchise
  model capital returns (dividends + buybacks)
- Carries ~$1.6B in long-term debt (~6.5x EBITDA) from securitization facilities
- Capital returned via dividends; no meaningful buyback program historically
- High leverage is standard for franchise-model companies (predictable royalty cash flows)
